All About Bunnies

Bunnies are social animals and love to have companions.

A bunny's diet primarily consists of hay, fresh vegetables, and clean water.

Bunnies have nearly 360-degree vision, allowing them to see predators coming.

Bunnies are known for their powerful hind legs, which help them hop and escape danger.

Bunnies can live up to 10 years with proper care and a healthy lifestyle.

Baby bunnies are called kits, and they are born with their eyes closed and without fur.

Bunnies communicate using body language, such as thumping their hind legs to warn of danger.

Bunnies groom themselves often, keeping their fur clean and soft.
